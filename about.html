Currently I am an audio engineer with 5+ years of experience looking to make a switch into the tech industry. I've always been interested in tech and am now pursuing that passion more professionally. After many years working in the music industry I have started to notice flaws in the softwares I have to use daily. This has ignited my curiosity for software development. With a new passion for making programs more efficient and user friendly, I started the Java Developer Bootcamp through Grand Circus in March of 2022. Although I am still in school, this program has already given me the opportunity to grow my technical skills in Java, HTML, and CSS while working individually and with a team. I love the sense of satisfaction I get when I complete a tough project. I believe my past experiences as an audio engineer mixed with my education through Grand Circus will allow me to one day be a every effective software developer. When I'm not in class or working I enjoy camping, hiking, running, and playing/writing music. I often have shows in the Metro Detroit Area with the many bands I play guitar for.
